About

Hi, I'm Maryam

I'm a Principal Software Engineer with a PhD in Computer Science and over 20 years of experience designing and developing complex software systems. I genuinely love what I do—building systems that solve real problems and making a meaningful impact in tech.

My journey has taken me through some fascinating domains. I've spent years working in finance tech and data tech, where I've built deep expertise in Spark, Databricks, Snowflake, SQL, and Large Language Models (LLMs). During my time at Morgan Stanley in trading tech and data, I got a firsthand understanding of the financial industry's unique challenges and requirements—knowledge I still draw on today.

Beyond trading floors and data pipelines, I've been passionate about solving data quality problems. I've contributed to building data quality tools and worked with clients across industries to address their specific data challenges. It's work that matters, and I'm proud of the solutions we've delivered together.

What drives me most is staying at the forefront of technology. I'm constantly learning, experimenting with new tools, and discovering best practices I can apply to deliver high-quality, scalable solutions. Whether it's optimizing data-driven applications, designing financial systems, or enhancing data quality processes, I bring that same enthusiasm to every project.

I'm also a big believer in knowledge sharing. I love collaborating with teams, mentoring others, and helping people grow. There's something special about working together to solve hard problems and seeing junior engineers develop into strong engineers themselves. If I can contribute to that journey for someone else, it makes the work even more rewarding.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to chat about software engineering, data systems, or anything tech-related. I'm always happy to connect.
